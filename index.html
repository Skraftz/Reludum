Reludum

Who is Reludum?

Reludum is a Indi dev who released two games in 2019. Copycat's Phenomenon and Letter [S]wap. He has disappeared off the internet since 2021.

Copycat's Phenomenon

Copycat's Phenomenon is a co-op horror fps where you and up to 17 friends are apart of the search-and-rescue team sent by the Black Ravens. The game is you going in and fighting off the different horrors as you collect folders and extract. With 4 classes with varying loudouts and 7 characters with different buffs and debuffs to decide, you and your team should have everything you need to survive, Right?

Letter [S]wap

Letter [S]wap is a short singleplayer horror game using a unique interaction system where you drag your mouse to select the options. The puzzle game acts like a adventure game with an fps camera and horror flavors. The strange controls and the constant tention makes for an enjoyable bit-sized game. When your old friend, Dr. K, asks you to meet after so long, figure out what has happened and watch out for what's to come!
